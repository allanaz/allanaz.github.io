I'm a software engineer with experience building tools for progressive organizations and democratic campaigns. Currently I'm a Full Stack Engineer at DSPolitical, where I work on digital advertising infrastructure that helps campaigns and causes reach voters at scale.

Before that, I was at NGP VAN, building digital organizing tools for campaigns and advocacy organizations. Projects included improving payment processing infrastructure with Stripe and adding new payment methods like Venmo and PayPal, advocacy tools for submitting public comments on government regulations, a customization tool for supporter-facing donation forms, and a refactoring of email unsubscribe workflows.

I studied Physics and Computer Science at MIT. Outside of class I worked with a congressional campaign, built telescope control software, and developed educational games for middle schoolers. I also helped run the largest undergraduate technology club on campus, leading a research competition for high school students.

I grew up in Oceanside, CA and currently live in Washington, DC. I enjoy photography, reading, and traveling as often as I can.
